# Biology Process Viz

## Use it for

- Cell structure and division
- Gene expression and inheritance
- Metabolic pathways, signaling, ecology

## Workflow

1. Establish the structural level first: organ, cell, organelle, pathway, or ecosystem role.
2. Animate stage changes or regulation flow on top of that stable structure.
3. Use directional arrows to separate activation, inhibition, transfer, or flow.
4. End with the functional consequence or phenotype.

## Guardrails

- Do not mix biological scales without explaining the jump.
- Preserve stage order and causal direction.
- Keep the closing frame focused on function, not only structure.
