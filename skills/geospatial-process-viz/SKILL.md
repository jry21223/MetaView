# Geospatial Process Viz

## Use it for

- Plate motion and landform evolution
- Water cycle, atmospheric or oceanic circulation
- Population migration and spatial change over time

## Workflow

1. Fix a stable base map or region frame before animating changes.
2. Express movement, flow, or deformation as directional change over time.
3. Keep regions comparable across frames by preserving the same spatial reference.
4. End with a region-level explanation of the pattern.

## Guardrails

- Keep orientation and scale readable.
- Do not change map reference frames mid-explanation.
- Compress dense spatial detail into region blocks when the frame would overload.
