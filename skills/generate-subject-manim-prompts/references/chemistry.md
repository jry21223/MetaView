# Chemistry Prompt Guidance

Use this file for molecules, reactions, bonding, electron movement, orbitals, equilibrium, and stoichiometric visuals.

## Priorities

- Define the representation first: ball-and-stick, structural formula, reaction scheme, orbital sketch, or concentration graph.
- Keep atom identity, bond identity, charge, and phase labels consistent throughout the scene.
- Show reaction changes as structured transitions, not random object replacements.
- Make conditions visible when they matter: heat, catalyst, solvent, pH, pressure, or light.

## Prompt Additions

- Ask for a species list with names, formulas, colors, and labels.
- Ask for before, during, and after states of the reaction or transformation.
- Require electron flow arrows or bond changes only when chemically meaningful.
- Ask for legends when color encodes atom type, phase, or concentration.
- Require the final frame to summarize what changed and why.

## Failure Patterns To Prevent

- Atoms changing identity because of color inconsistency
- Bonds appearing or disappearing without explanation
- Reaction conditions omitted even though they affect interpretation
- Mixing microscopic structure and macroscopic graph views without transition
