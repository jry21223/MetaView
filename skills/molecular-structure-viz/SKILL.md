# Molecular Structure Viz

## Use it for

- Molecular geometry and bond structure
- Reaction mechanisms and transition states
- Orbital or structural rearrangement explanations

## Workflow

1. Build the initial structure with explicit atoms, bonds, and key functional groups.
2. Animate bond breaking, bond forming, and structural rearrangement as separate steps.
3. Attach narration at the exact frames where energy barriers, catalysts, or conditions matter.
4. Close with products and mechanism summary.

## Guardrails

- Keep valence and stoichiometric consistency.
- Do not hide key intermediates if they explain the mechanism.
- Separate structure change from explanatory text when a frame is crowded.
